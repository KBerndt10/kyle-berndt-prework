About Me

I am from Savage, MN. I attended the Twin Cities University of Minnesota's Twin City campus to earn a bachelor's degree in computer science. During high school I spent the summers as a voluntyeer counselor at a boys camp caled Camp Nathanael in Hinckley, MN. During that time I taught various skills including, archery, model rocketry, and geocaching. During the summer of 2018 I had the opportunity to work at Wings Financial Credit Union as a web developer intern. My big accomplishment during the summer was to create a significant portion of their online loan application form.

One of my most unique hobbies is aquarium keeping. I currently keep about 250 gallons of freshwater aquariums which contain over 60 fish, 7 varieties of plants, and numerous snails. I also have a 75 gallon reef aquarium, which has snails, hermit crabs, and about 25 types of coral. My favorite species of fish is the stripe-tailed eartheater cichlid (Geophagus Winemilleri).

I also enjoy playing both physical and digital games. I play most of my video games on Xbox. I enjoy a broad spectrum of video games, but some of my favorites would probably the Gears of War series, Terraria, and the Mount and Blade series. I enjoy playing a variety of physical games as well, and I even worked as a seasonal sales representative at Games By James in 2014. I am currently the game master for a Fantasycraft campaign, which is an RPG game similar to Dungeons and Dragons.
